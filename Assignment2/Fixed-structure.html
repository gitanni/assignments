Exploring Bend, Oregon

Map 1:

This is the location of where Bend is in Oregon. It is apart of Central Oregon, where the weather is often sunny and dry. Bend has a population of approximately 103k. There are many activities to do within Bend.

Map 2:

One of the actitivties in Bend is the Old Mill District. This district is a shopper center that was once a mill back in 1916. Today, there are various shops, restaurants, and movie theather that lie within this mill.

Map 3:

Another activity is floating down the Riverbend Park. You can bring your own floaties to float down the gorgeous Deschutes River or rent a floatie for day from a rental shop in the Old Mill District. If you are not wanting to float, you can visit the Riverbend Park and walk on the path or do a BBQ day!
